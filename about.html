Our Story

Safaris Unravelled (pty) Ltd is a food and hospitality business based in Selebi Phikwe, Botswana. Since the day we opened our doors, we have grown to become a trusted name in the community serving the residents of Selebi Phikwe and surrounding areas with our delicious food and excellent service.

We specialize in restaurant dining, custom cake orders and catering services for all types of occasions ranging from birthdays to corporate events. Every meal we serve and cake we bake is made with the utmost care and with the finest ingridieents to ensure a memorable experience for our customers.

Meet Our Owner

Safaris Unravelled was founded by a passionate entrepreneur with a love for for food and hospitality. With years of expirience in the industry the owner built the restaurant from the ground up.H e began with custom cake orders and later expanding to a full-service restaurant and catering.

Today the founder continues to lead Safaris U nravelled with the same passion that inspired it's founding, ensuring that every dish served and cake delivered meets the highest standards of quality and taste.
