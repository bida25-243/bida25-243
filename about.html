Restaurant Story

Safaris Uravelled (Pty) Ltd, trading as Markab Cakes and Restaurant, is a restaurant and catering business based in Selebi Phikwe, Botswana. We have have grown to become a trusted name in the community, serving residents of Selebi Phikwe and surrounding areas such as Mmadinare with quality food and exceptional service. We specialise in restaurant dining, custom cake orders and catering services for events of all sizes. Every meal we prepare and serve is made with care and quality ingredients this includes our custom cakes.

We here at Safaris Uravelled believe in good food, friendly service and creating memorable experiences with every meal served for our customers during every visit. Whether you are dining in our restaurant, ordering a custom cake or booking our catering services, we garantee you will receive the best quality and service. We are proud to be a part of the Selebi Phikwe community and look forward to serving you for many years to come.

The Owner of Safaris Uravelled
